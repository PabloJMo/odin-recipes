Best Scrambled Eggs

Description

Easy and filling, this meaty, cheesy scrambled egg recipe will carry you through many mornings (and breakfast-for-dinners) to come.

This best scrambled egg recipe took a bit of tweaking, but I've finally made the best scrambled eggs ever! Yours friends and family will love it.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 thin slices cooked ham, diced (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons sliced Cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream
  • Â½ teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs in a small bowl until smooth.
  2. Mix in ham, Cheddar cheese, and heavy cream.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.

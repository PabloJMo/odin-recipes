Juicy Roasted Chicken

Description

Every home chef needs a roast chicken recipe in their back pocket, and this one results in the juiciest chicken of all. With just five ingredients.

Roasted chicken never fails to impress, but it's surprisingly simple to make! This juicy roasted chicken recipe is perfect for beginner cooks and old pros alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, giblets removed
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder, or to taste
  • Â½ cup butter or margarine
  • 1 stalk celery, leaves removed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Season the chicken inside and out.
  3. Add the butter to the chicken and the pan.
  4. Stuff the cavity with celery.
  5. Bake until the chicken is fully roasted.
